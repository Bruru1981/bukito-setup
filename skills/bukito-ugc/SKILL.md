# Bukito UGC — User-Generated Content Curation

## Why UGC Matters
- Customer photos drive 4x higher conversions than branded content
- Consumers find UGC 9.8x more impactful than influencer content for restaurant decisions
- UGC builds community — guests become part of the Bukito story

## Hashtags to Monitor
- `#Bukito` — primary
- `#BukitoSumbawa` — location-specific
- `#ParadiseWithFangs` — tagline
- `#Kertasari` — village
- `#SumbawaSurf` — surf community crossover

## Curation Workflow

1. **Discover** — Search hashtags weekly on Instagram/TikTok
2. **Select** — Pick photos that match Bukito's warm, analog aesthetic
3. **Request Permission** — DM the creator using templates below
4. **Design** — Create branded repost using Paper MCP template
5. **Publish** — Post with credit via Postiz
6. **Track** — Log in content_analytics with post_type: 'ugc'

## Permission Request Templates

### English
```
Hey [name]! We love this shot of Bukito! Would you mind if we share it on our feed? We'll tag you of course. Thanks!
```

### Bahasa Indonesia
```
Hai [nama]! Kami suka banget foto Bukito-nya! Boleh nggak kalau kami share di feed kami? Pasti kami tag ya. Makasih!
```

### After Permission Granted
```
Amazing, thank you! We'll post it this week and tag you. Welcome back anytime!
```

```
Footer:   "BUKITO" left / "#PARADISEWITHFANGS" right, Kisrre 11px
```

## Selection Criteria (what makes good UGC)

### Yes — Repost These
- Warm, natural lighting
- Shows food, atmosphere, or location authentically
- Captures the Bukito vibe (relaxed, tropical, adventurous)
- Good composition (doesn't need to be perfect)
- Shows real moments — laughing, eating, surfing

### No — Skip These
- Blurry, dark, or heavily filtered
- Shows other brand logos prominently
- Negative context or complaints
- Screenshots or memes
- Content that doesn't match brand values
