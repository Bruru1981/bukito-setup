## Standing decisions

- **Menu prices integrated into dish names** — no separate price column / no price scanning. Keeps the A5 menu calm and editorial.
- **Kisrre Regular is the default for ~90% of work**, ALL CAPS, tight tracking. Secondary fonts only with explicit justification; never all three fonts in one piece.
- **One accent colour per piece.** Prices are always Jungle Green (#008134).
- **Website animations are editorial, not SaaS** — subtle, scroll-triggered, generous padding. Never flashy.
- **Photos are Kristof-grade or graded to match** (Golden Standard LUT at 70–85%, never 100%).
